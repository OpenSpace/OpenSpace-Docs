# Solar Browsing
The solar browsing feature in OpenSpace enables visualization of time-sequenced solar observations from spacecraft such as SDO, STEREO, and SOHO. Images are downloaded from the [Helioviewer](https://helioviewer.org/) service and can be displayed either as an image plane attached to the observing spacecraft or projected directly onto the solar surface.

This section covers how to download and prepare the image data and how to set up the renderables in OpenSpace.
